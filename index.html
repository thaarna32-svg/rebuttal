How to use Rebuttal
A complete guide for owners, coaches, and debaters
Getting started
1
Get your Anthropic API key
Go to console.anthropic.com, sign up, and copy your API key. Paste it in the AI Feedback page.
2
Create your teams
Go to Teams & Coaches. Add each debate team with a coach name and location.
3
Add debate topics
Build a bank of motions in the Topics page and schedule upcoming debates.
4
Record a debate
Go to Record Debate, tap the mic button, allow microphone access, label and save your recording.
5
Get AI feedback
Go to AI Feedback, select your recording, and tap Analyse. Claude will transcribe and score the debate.
Features at a glance
Database
All data is saved to your Supabase Postgres database — accessible from any device, anywhere.
AI analysis
Powered by Claude (Anthropic). Scores argument clarity, evidence use, rebuttal skill, and delivery.
Group chats
Create channels per team or topic. All messages are saved to the database.
Subscribers
Enrol members and export the full list as a CSV spreadsheet at any time.
